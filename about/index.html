About

2017-10-24 · 64 words · 1 minute read

The UMMC Bioinformatics Journal Club Blog provides a monthly discussion forum for relevant topics, journal articles, research projects, and conferences in the context of computational biology, data science, clinical research, and basic science research.

Objectives

  • Promote informal interactions between Bioinformatics enthusiasts
  • Expose students and researchers to current topics and tools in Bioinformatics
  • Foster interdepartmental collaborations
